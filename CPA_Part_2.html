Job Title

Financial Analyst - CPA Designation Preferred

Qualifications Required

  • CPA designation (or in progress)
  • Strong background in accounting, finance, and tax
  • Proficiency in financial modeling and analysis tools (Excel, SAP, QuickBooks)

Duties and Responsibilities

  • Prepare and analyze financial reports
  • Monitor budgets and forecasts
  • Ensure compliance with financial regulations
  • Assist in tax planning and auditing processes

Compensation

  • Salary: $70,000 - $90,000 annually (entry-level)
  • Benefits:
    • Medical/dental coverage
    • Paid vacation and sick leave
    • Professional development reimbursement
