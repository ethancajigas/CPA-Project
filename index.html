CPA Certification

Steps to CPA Designation

Education:

  • Completed CPA Professional Education Program (CPA PEP):
    • Core Modules: Financial Reporting, Management Accounting, Audit, Finance, Taxation
    • Elective Modules: Assurance, Taxation
    • Capstone Modules: Leadership Skills, Integration of Competencies

Examination:

  • Passed the Common Final Examination (CFE)

Practical Experience:

  • Completed 30 months of relevant work experience
  • Up to 12 months of prior experience may count
